## what this site is

Not a company blog. Not a newsletter. A public working notebook, organized into three kinds of artifact:

- **Field notes** — short, dated posts about a specific thing I've figured out or am stuck on
- **Working papers** — longer drafts I plan to submit or post to arXiv / SSRN
- **Series** — multi-episode threads on a single question, numbered and finite

## how to read it

Use the **tags** if you came here for a topic. Use the **archive** if you came here for a period. Use the **series** tab if you want the long-form version of a thread. The search box works on titles and body text.

Every post is marked **EN** or **KO** in the meta line. A handful of posts have both; they are cross-linked at the bottom of each.
